Material World

  • High Concept: Platformer Puzzle, where the tilemap is a bunch of different materials that react differently to the ball - realistic physics.
  • Player Experience Goals: Accomplishment, frustration, curiousity/exploration
  • Genre: Platformer
  • Game play: Arrow keys to move the camera, mouse to generate force that moves the ball
  • Prototype goal: Tilemaps and P2 materials.
  • Prototype Assets: The music is #9 by Moon Hooch. Everything else, I made.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Starstruck?
